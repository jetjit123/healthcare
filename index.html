Our Scanners

Philips Ingenia Evolution 1.5T

  • MRI scanner designed to provide high-quality imaging and improved patient comfort.
  • It features a range of advanced imaging technologies, including dStream digital broadband technology and Compressed SENSE acceleration, which allow for faster and more accurate imaging with reduced noise.
  • The Ingenia Evolution 1.5T is equipped with patient-centered features such as a wide bore, ambient lighting, and noise-reducing technology stack improves patient comfort.

Siemens Magnetom Sola

  • MRI Scanner that is designed to provide fast, accurate, and reliable imaging.
  • It features a powerful 70 cm bore, which can accommodate a wide range of patient sizes and shapes, as well as advanced imaging technologies, such as BioMatrix and Tim 4G.
  • The BioMatrix technology adapts to the individual patient's anatomy and physiology, allowing for improved image quality.

Celesteion Purevision PET/CT

  • Medical imaging system that combines Positron Emission Tomography (PET) and Computed Tomography (CT) technologies. It is designed to provide physicians with highly detailed images of internal body structures and functions, which helps in diagnosing and monitoring various medical conditions.
  • Faster scan times, and a lower radiation dose for patients.
