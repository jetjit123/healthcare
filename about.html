Company Profile

At Medetick, we have managed to combine the provision of both the scanner as well as the staffing solution tailored to each hospital or site, be it within the NHS or the private sector.

Our primary goal is to optimize existing resources within your trust, resulting in increased productivity, reduced waiting lists, and the prevention of scanner closures due to staff shortages.

We provide a core team for each site, that is dedicatedly working at your hospital or site. This enables better understanding of the local protocols and policies specific to your site. The outcome of this is continued consistency of work as well as optimisation of workflow.

Our flexibility is a key strength, as we can provide staffing for 12-hour shifts or longer, 7 days a week. We are also ready to cover ad-hoc shifts and accommodate short-notice absences when needed.


"Trust Us To Meet Your Staffing Requirements Reliably And Professionally."

What We Offer

  Locally Available Radiographers

Medetick has a team of experienced radiographers who live locally and this can provide cost savings to healthcare providers, resulting in better quality and more efficient services. Additionally, experienced local radiographers have a better understanding of the hospital's protocols and expectations, which can lead to a better patient experience and increased patient safety. Furthermore, experienced local radiographers can deal better with emergencies, resulting in less waiting time for patients.

  UK Trained Radiographers

Our UK-trained radiographers prioritize meeting industry standards and adhering to best practices, ensuring the delivery of high-quality patient care. With their familiarity with the UK healthcare system, they seamlessly integrate into healthcare facilities and collaborate effectively with other healthcare professionals. Moreover, their deep understanding of the local culture and patient needs within the UK enables them to provide culturally sensitive care, fostering better communication and enhancing patient satisfaction. Radiographers trained in the UK also comply with the professional standards and codes of conduct set by esteemed regulatory bodies like the Health and Care Professions Council (HCPC),ICO and CDC.

  Financial Benefits

The implementation of experienced radiographers will save the hospital money in several ways. The cost of training new staff will be significantly reduced as we will be utilizing experienced radiographers who are familiar with our hospital's protocols and expectations. Additionally, the cost of purchasing any other scanning service will be avoided as we will be using the existing capacity (static scanner in the hospital). This will result in a significant cost benefit to the hospital.

Our radiographers can perform all modalities of scans, including MR and CT scans. This flexibility allows for a better quality of service, which can lead to better patient outcomes and improved patient satisfaction. Additionally, the use of experienced local radiographers can be reviewed annually, and the existing capacity can be shown in a better light, resulting in continued cost savings and better quality services.

  Advantages of Using Existing Capacity

We will be utilizing our existing capacity (static scanner in the hospital) for the radiography services. This will not only save us the cost of purchasing any other scanning service but also ensure that we are fully utilizing our resources. The radiography services will be able to do any scan, including MR and CT, making them a valuable asset to the hospital.

  Better Efficiency in WorkFlows

The implementation of our experienced radiographers who are familiar with protocols will show a significant increase in efficiency and productivity in workflows and processes. The radiology department will see a significant increase in revenue as they will be able to perform more scans. The emergency department will also benefit from the radiography services as they will be able to quickly and easily access the services during emergency situations and less time is wasted on vetting and training.

  Core Team

The scanner services will be staffed by experienced radiographers who are familiar with our hospital's protocols and expectations.
This will reduce the need for training and ensure that our patients receive the highest quality care. Medetick will create a core team of radiographers who will work at the hospital regularly and will be available throughout.

  No Discrepancy in Service Levels

There will be no discrepancy in the work as the radiography services will be staffed by core team of experienced radiographers who are familiar with the hospital's protocols and expectations. This will ensure that the patients receive consistent and high-quality care.
